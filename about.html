About Us

Established in 2024, Electronica was introduced and developed by a young entrepreneur from Balsall Heath, Birmingham called Zain Ahmed. He was tired of all these other companies providing little to no care for customer satisfaction as they only cared about gaining a sale. This is when Electronica was made.

Together with his half brother Anwar Jibawi, the brothers then went onto purchasing products like phones or laptops from local sellers for a cheap price and then selling these products to family members. Once word spread around, the brothers then went onto opening a small hut-like shop on Coventry Rd which led to the Electronica empire which reigns over the United Kingdom as the top 5 electronic suppliers worldwide!
